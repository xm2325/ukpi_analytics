One communication plan is not enough for the simulated investor book.

The workflow creates 5 interpretable customer segments from 2,500 synthetic customers. The strongest simulated segment-offer result is 25.1 pp for CASH_TO_INVEST_EDUCATION in Cash-heavy cautious investors.

The output supports service design and education routing. It does not recommend a fund, portfolio, risk level, or personal investment action.

Business question: how many customers are in each segment?

How to read it

  • Start with segment size before interpreting offer performance.
  • A small segment can show high lift but still have limited operational impact.
  • A large segment with moderate lift may be more useful for a scaled campaign.

What I would and would not claim
